Room-by-room guide of the barn

The guided tour

The barn is a painstakingly restored 3 en-suite bedroom house with a well appointed, hand made kitchen, sitting, dining room and extra wc. Handmade Moroccan terracotta floor tiles in all rooms, complete with under-floor heating or cooling in every room. It comfortably sleeps 6 adults and is child friendly.

Living & Dining Room

The main room of the barn is 6m x 11m, open to the rafters, and is divided into a dining room and sitting area. Leather sofas and club chairs surround the open fireplace and is the ideal lounging area for reading or watching television.

A large bookcase contains dozens of volumes and guests are encouraged to borrow anything of interest. Both French and English satellite stations can be viewed on the 32" wide screen television, which has a DVD player connected; and the Radio / CD player is equipped with a iPod docking point for playing your own music.

The solid wood dining table seats 12 and crockery, cutlery and glassware for 12 place settings is provided. A games cupboard is stocked with plenty of family amusements and the sit-up bar (stools provided!) separates the dining room from the kitchen.

Kitchen

The custom-made kitchen is traditionally finished with oak worktops, a Belfast sink and a fully operational wood-burning stove. All modern essentials are provided including an electric oven and hob, dishwasher, two fridges, freezer, toaster, kettle and coffee pot. A multitude of pots, pans, roasting trays and cooking utensils are also available.

French windows from the kitchen open onto a private terrace which overlooks the pool and garden.

Master Bedroom

The master bedroom has been designed as a haven of indulgence. Furnished with a super-king four-poster bed in solid wood, the room contains matching dresser, wardrobe and bedside tables. The room has its own en-suite bathroom fitted with both a bath and separate walk-in rain shower; heated towel rail, WC, wash basin and mirror.

Second Bedroom

Perfectly designed, this room has a queen size bed and matching wardrobe, dresser and bedside tables. The room's sizeable en-suite bathroom has rain shower, heated towel rail, WC, wash basin, mirror and plenty of storage space for the essential toiletries and cosmetics. The room has an outside door which opens onto a private patio overlooking the pool and garden.

Third Bedroom

This room is arranged with twin beds, which can become a double if required. It contains the identical top quality furniture and en-suite facilities as the second bedroom and also has access through patio doors to the main driveway.

Guest Bathroom

In addition to the en-suite facilities provided in each bedroom, an extra WC with wash basin is located off the hallway.

Laundry Room

Finally, a utility room houses the washing machine/tumble dryer, iron and ironing board, pool towels and a change of bed linen as well as all the necessary cleaning products.

Swimming Pool

The 12 by 6 metre swimming pool is located in a secluded spot behind the main house for maximum privacy. The pool is salt water chlorinated which is good for the environment and those with alergies. It is equipped with automatic hard cover, lights and alarms to ensure safety and security.
