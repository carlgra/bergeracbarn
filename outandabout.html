  Below is a sample of the local amenities, more details can be found in the Visitors' Handbook.

Banks

There are a number of banks in Bergerac town centre which should provide for most banking needs. These include Barclays at 30 Place Gambetta, Bergerac, Credit Agricole at 27, Rue Ste Catherine, Bergerac and Banque Populaire at 19, Rue Ste Catherine, Bergerac.

Further details including maps and telephone numbers can be found in the Visitors' Handbook.

Markets

One of the great pleasures of staying in this part of France is the opportunity to do your shopping at the outdoor markets in the local towns. Our favourite markets are detailed below:

Issegeac - Sunday Mornings throughout the year and Night Markets on Thursday evenings in July and August

Bergerac - Wednesday and Saturday mornings, Gourmet Markets on Wednesday evenings in July and August

Many more markets can be found on the Tourist office web site here - here

Tourist Office

The local tourism office is located at 97 rue Neuve d'Argenson, Bergerac. They speak English, French, German, Spanish and Italian and can be found on the web here or contacted via phone on +33(0)5.53.57.03.11.
