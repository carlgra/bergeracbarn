Room-by-room guide of the farmhouse

The guided tour

The farmhouse is a lovingly restored 3 bedroom cottage with a well appointed kitchen, bathroom and sitting/dining room. It comfortably sleeps 6 people.

Kitchen

The handmade kitchen is fully equipped and has an oven, hob and microwave, washer/dryer, fridge/freezer, coffee machine. It also has a wood burning range for those cold winter evenings. Everything you need to cook and serve a great meal including limoges crockery, le crueset oven-ware and cutlery for six people is supplied.

Sitting & dining room

The sitting/dining room has two and three seater sofas and a huge open fireplace. The large monastry table seats eight comfortably on benches, perfect for that rustic feast.

Master Bedroom

The master bedroom has a king-size bed, chest of drawers, wardrobe and all linen is of the highest quality.

Second Bedroom

The second bedroom contains two single beds with fitted wardrobes and has a Juliet balcony that overlooks the courtyard.

Third Bedroom

The third bedroom contains a queen size bed with fitted wardrobes and has a patio door that leads to the covered terrase outside.

Bathroom

The bathroom has been tastefully finished with a bath, separate shower, toilet and basin, complete with under-floor heating.

Covered Terrace

The covered terrace adjoining the farmhouse has cushioned seating with a dining table and built in barbeque for those balmy summer nights.
